At White Water Rafting, our passion is to provide unforgettable experiences of adventure and excitement on the white waters of Mato Grosso, Brazil.

History

Welcome to "White Water Rafting", where adrenaline meets nature in its purest form! Our rafting company was born out of a love for the sport and a passion for sharing unforgettable experiences. Today, our team is made up of experienced and passionate guides who know every bend and current of the river. We are committed to safety and fun, providing adventures that range from peaceful family outings to intense expeditions for the bravest.
